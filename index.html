LiberationValues是什么？

LiberationValues一个针对反威权主义左派的政治测试，旨在为一个人在14种不同的政治价值观上的观点分配百分比。你将会看到一些观点，然后需要选择对这些观点的态度，从非常同意到非常不同意中每个选项都将影响你的测试结果。在测试结束时，你的答案将与每个值的可能最大值进行比较，从而得出一个显示在坐标轴上的百分比。请诚实作答！

测试中有哪十四项价值观？


温和

温和的自由意志左派比其他象限的人相信更大的国家，他们也可能相信把资本主义国家改革成福利国家是可行的。

激进轴

激进

激进的自由意志左派极端反对国家和资本主义，他们寻求一个没有不公正等级制度的世界。

左翼联合

左翼联合派认为，无政府资本主义不值得信任，自由意志社会主义者应该与马克思主义者，甚至是马克思列宁主义者结盟。

合作轴

自由意志联合

自由意志联合派相信左派和右派有一个共同的目标，那就是建立一个无国家的社会。因此，他们认为马克思列宁主义本身就违背了他们的信仰。

CENTRALIZED

Centralists believe that state ownership is the best way to gradually transforms capitalism.

OWNERSHIP

DECENTRALIZED

Decentralists reject state ownership and advocate for communal or workers ownership.

LOCALIST

Localists believe in Isolation from the world, or putting your own community above oworld issue and defend it from foreign capitalists.

WORLD

GLOBALIST

Globalists believe that all nations must reject borders and unite into one World union.

TRADITIONALIST

Traditionalists value preservation of culture above all forms of social progress deeming it to be a fundamental building block of society that should not be erased.

CULTURE

PROGRESSIVE

Social progressives value the liberation of queer individuals and historically opressed ethnical groups above preservation of culture.

REFORM

Reformists value gradualism as a viable option to achieve socialism, while extreme reformists might even reject protest or direct action.

REBELLIOUSNESS

REVOLUTION

Revolutionairies think reform is an illusion, and strive for revolution anytime it's possible.

MARKETS

Marketeers are a vague group that support Markets as the main mode of production, while Social Democrats, or Social Libertarians believe in a fairer capitalism, Market Anarchists believe that Markets aren't inherently capitalistic

MARKETS

PLANNING

Advocaters for decentral planning believe that it's more efficient and less bureaucratic than central planning, while central planning is top-down, decentral is bottom-up.
